📌 Rules

13 Rules — Click to expand ▼
1. Never give up easy. Every objection gets one strong push. They hang up — you don't. Exception: "don't call again" = legal, stop.
2. Never read word-for-word. Talk like a real person.
3. Always push the demo number. "Let me text you the number" = your default close.
4. Say "phone answering service." Not AI. Unless they ask.
5. If they ask about AI, be honest. Then redirect to demo.
6. After the pitch, STOP talking. Silence is powerful.
7. Speak slowly and confidently. Calm, direct. Like you know something they don't.
8. After they agree, text IMMEDIATELY and hang up. Ending fast = confidence.
9. "Not interested" = don't soften. Go straight to the problem. No "I hear you."
10. Log every call. Answered/not, response, took number yes/no, notes.
11. Never call before 8 AM or after 5:30 PM in their time zone.
12. Never call Monday or Friday.
13. You are not begging. You have something that makes them money. Their loss if they can't see it.
